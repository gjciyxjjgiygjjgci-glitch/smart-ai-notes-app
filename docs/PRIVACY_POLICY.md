# Privacy Policy for Smart AI Notes

**Last Updated:** December 17, 2024

## Introduction

Smart AI Notes ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information
- Email address
- Name
- Profile picture (optional)
- Authentication credentials

### 2. Audio Recordings
- Voice recordings of lectures
- Audio file metadata (duration, format, size)
- Transcribed text from recordings

### 3. Usage Data
- App usage statistics
- Feature interactions
- Error logs and crash reports
- Device information (model, OS version)

### 4. Notes and Content
- Study notes you create
- Subject tags and organization
- Summaries generated
- Export history

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

1. **Provide Services**
   - Transcribe audio to text
   - Generate AI summaries
   - Store and organize your notes
   - Enable sharing and export features

2. **Improve Experience**
   - Enhance transcription accuracy
   - Optimize AI models
   - Fix bugs and improve performance
   - Develop new features

3. **Communication**
   - Send service updates
   - Respond to support requests
   - Notify about new features
   - Share educational content (with consent)

4. **Analytics**
   - Understand usage patterns
   - Measure feature adoption
   - Improve user experience

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage
- Audio files: Firebase Cloud Storage (encrypted)
- Notes and metadata: Cloud Firestore (encrypted)
- User authentication: Firebase Authentication

### Security Measures
- End-to-end encryption for data transmission
- Secure cloud storage with access controls
- Regular security audits
- Automatic logout after inactivity
- Two-factor authentication (optional)

### Data Retention
- Active accounts: Data retained indefinitely
- Deleted accounts: Data removed within 30 days
- Audio files: Automatically deleted after 90 days (configurable)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

1. **Access** - Request copies of your data
2. **Correction** - Update incorrect information
3. **Deletion** - Delete your account and data
4. **Export** - Download your data in portable format
5. **Opt-out** - Disable analytics and marketing communications

### How to Exercise Rights
- In-app: Settings → Privacy → Data Management
- Email: privacy@smartainotes.com
- Response time: Within 30 days

## Children's Privacy

Smart AI Notes is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such data, contact us immediately.

## Data Sharing

We **DO NOT**:
- Sell your personal data
- Share data with advertisers (beyond anonymized analytics)
- Use your content for training without consent

We **MAY** share data:
- With your explicit consent
- To comply with legal obligations
- To protect our rights and safety
- With service providers (under strict agreements)
